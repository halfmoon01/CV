Overview

Biz-News is an AI-powered news summary and recommendation service developed during the Kakao Tech Bootcamp Hackathon in February 2025. The project won 2nd place in the competition.

Using Graph RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology with Neo4j graph database, the service provides personalized news recommendations and intelligent summaries based on complex relationships between news articles, topics, and user interests.
